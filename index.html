Instructions:

Time to play Poly-Glot! Spell words using the letters in the polygons, but watch out: words must be at least 4 letters long, and all words must contain the central letter. Letters may be used more than once.

Four-letter words are worth only 1 point, but longer words are worth 1 point per letter. If your word contains all the available letters, you get bonus points equal to the number of shapes in the grid.

You can spell words by typing or clicking on the letters, and you can also refresh the view to see the letters in a different arrangement.
