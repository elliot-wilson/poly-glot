Poly-Glot

Instructions

Time to play Poly-Glot! Spell words using the letters in the polygons, but watch out: words must be at least 4 letters long, and all words must contain the central letter. Four-letter words are worth only 1 point, but longer words are worth 1 point per letter. If your word contains all the available letters, you get bonus points equal to the number of shapes in the grid.
